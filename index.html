About Event

MES Mampad College is bringing back it's most prestigious event VOKAZA, South Indian Management Meet. VOKAZA in it's Fifth edition is going to be a hub of eccentric oppurtunity for the students from divergent background to showcase and expolit their talents and to gain the best of encouragement in the best of ways

Our College

Dr. Gafoor Memorial MES Mampad College was established in the year 1965. It is the very first autonomous college to situate in Malappuram. The college is accredited by NAAC. The college offers finest education on 19 courses across 5 streams namely science,commerce and banking, management, media and mass communication, arts etc. The college also provides room for research and innovation.

Our Department

Department of Commerce is the largest department in our college. There are around 500 students studying at undergraduate and post graduate level in various course such as B.Com Co-operation, B.Com Computer Application and M.Com. Through persistent effort and enhancement, the department has carved its niche in the academic circle. A blend of conferences and workshops organized by the department offer enchanted opportunities for students, research scholars to strengthen and expand the knowledge, thereby enriching the students academically and non-academically. The department has been recognized as a research center since 2017. The department annually conducts Management Meets entitled VOKAZA to explore the managerial skill of the students. The meet had been conducted 4 times over the years.

General guidelines

  • Participation is open to all UG and PG students and also students from various Institutions.
  • All participants should report at the registration desk by 9:00 am at the venue on March 17th.
  • Spot registration time is between 9:00 am and 10:30 am
  • Online Registration through Google form is also available
  • Register your college/institution online in a minimum of 5 events (5 different teams) on or before 16th March 2023 to avail a 15% off on your total registration fee.
  • The Number of Registration slots for each event is limited
  • Participants should produce college/institution identity cards at the time of spot registration.
  • Mobile phones are strictly restricted during the time of events.
  • The participants are requested to maintain the decorum of the institution.
  • Team must be comprised of participants from the same college.
  • Participants of one event cannot participate in the other events except for spot events.
  • Decision of jury and committee will be final and no appeals will be entertained.
  • Lunch and refreshment will be provided on the day of event (except for Spot Games, Mehendi & Face Painting)
  • Every event has separate set of rules and all are required to abide by them
  • Participants who are found indulging in any malpractices shall be disqualified without notice.
  • Registration fee will not be refunded under any circumstances
  • Particular Events may be cancelled if there aren't a minimum of 5 Participants/Teams
